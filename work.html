Financial Services UX Research Stakeholder Management

Designing for financial services

Two projects across NatWest, RBS and Clydesdale Bank — one focused on eliminating friction from high-stakes application forms, the other on reimagining what a payment could feel like.

NatWest · RBS · Clydesdale Bank

The Problem

Legacy flows built around business logic rather than user understanding. Drop-off happening for reasons analytics couldn't explain — until we put real people in front of the journeys.

The Approach

Research first, always. Modular journey design, progressive disclosure and empathetic content strategy for RBS. Self-initiated Quick Pay concept for Clydesdale — a payment as fast as a tweet.

The Outcome

+12% uplift in credit card applications. +10% rise in approvals. Became an innovation partner for Clydesdale. First Quick Pay concept of its kind in fintech.

My Role

Lead UX, user research, journey design, prototyping, stakeholder management, concept design.

+12% applications +10% approvals Innovation partner
